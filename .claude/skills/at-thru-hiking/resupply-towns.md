### Service Legend
- **G** = Grocery store
- **O** = Outfitter
- **H** = Hostel
- **P** = Post Office
- **R** = Restaurant
- **L** = Laundry
- **$** = ATM/Bank

## 100-Mile Wilderness Planning

**Mile 2079 (Monson) to Mile 2183 (Baxter SP)**

| Option | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Full Carry** | 8-10 days food from Monson, heaviest but most common |
| **White House Landing** | Mile 2133, accessible by boat ($2 horn + boat fee), breaks carry in half |
| **Shaw's Drop** | Shaw's offers food drop service to White's Crossing |

**Food Math:**
- Days: 7-10 (depending on pace)
- Food weight: 1.5-2 lb/day
- Total carry: 10-20 lb of food

## Mail Drop Strategy

### Recommended Mail Drop Locations

| Location | Mile | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| Fontana Dam | 164 | Before Smokies |
| Damascus | 471 | Reliable, trail town |
| Waynesboro | 865 | Before Shenandoah |
| Harpers Ferry | 1026 | ATC can hold |
| Delaware Water Gap | 1298 | Church hostel holds |
| Hanover | 1755 | Co-op holds |
| Gorham | 1898 | Before Maine |
| Monson | 2079 | Before 100MW |

### Mail Drop Tips

1. **Call ahead** - Verify PO/hostel will hold packages
2. **Address properly** - Include "AT Thru-Hiker" and ETA
3. **Don't over-plan** - Buy as you go is often easier
4. **Special diets only** - Mail drops mainly for dietary needs
