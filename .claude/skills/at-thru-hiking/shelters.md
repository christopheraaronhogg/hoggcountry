## Shelter Etiquette

1. **First come, first served** - No reservations (except GSMNP)
2. **Share space** - Make room for late arrivals
3. **Quiet hours** - Generally 10 PM - 6 PM
4. **Hang food** - Use cables/bear boxes provided
5. **Pack out trash** - Leave no trace
6. **Don't hog space** - One person = one spot
7. **Tent if full** - Tenting near shelters usually allowed
