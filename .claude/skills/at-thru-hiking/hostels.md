## Hostel Etiquette

1. **Call ahead** - Verify availability, especially peak season
2. **Quiet hours** - Respect 10 PM - 7 AM typically
3. **Clean up** - Leave common areas tidy
4. **Tip shuttles** - Drivers often work on tips
5. **Share space** - Don't spread out unnecessarily
6. **Pay promptly** - Cash often preferred
7. **Sign guestbooks** - Part of the tradition

---

## Budget Planning

### Typical Hostel Costs

| Type | Range |
|------|-------|
| Tenting | $10-20/night |
| Bunk | $25-50/night |
| Private | $50-150/night |
| Work-for-stay | Free (limited availability) |

### Money-Saving Tips

1. **Tent when possible** - Hostels often have cheaper tent sites
2. **Work-for-stay** - Some hostels offer work exchanges
3. **Donation-based** - Several church hostels are donation only
4. **Split privates** - Share rooms with hiking partners
5. **Off-season** - Prices sometimes lower early/late season
